# Easy

## Matching {x, y} Repetitions

`^[0-9]{1,2}[A-Za-z]{3,}[.]{,3}$`

## Matching {x} Repetitions

`^[A-Za-z02468]{40}[13579\s]{5}$`

## Matching Zero Or More Repetitions

`^[0-9]{2,}[a-z]*[A-Z]*$`

## Matching One Or More Repetitions

`^[0-9]+[A-Z]+[a-z]+$`

## Matching Ending Items

`^[A-Za-z]*s$`
